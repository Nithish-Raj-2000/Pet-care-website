Frequently Asked Questions

For the safety of all pets, we require up-to-date Rabies, Distemper, and Bordetella vaccinations for dogs. Cats must have current FVRCP and Rabies vaccines. Proof of vaccination must be provided prior to check-in.

While we provide high-quality, easily digestible house food, we highly recommend bringing your pet's regular food to prevent any gastrointestinal upset caused by a sudden change in diet.

Yes! Our premium boarding suites come equipped with 24/7 webcams that you can access through our mobile app, allowing you to check in on your furry friend anytime.

A standard grooming session takes between 2 to 4 hours depending on the size of your pet, the condition of their coat, and the specific services requested. We will text you 30 minutes before they are ready for pickup.

We exclusively use positive reinforcement techniques. Our trainers focus on rewarding good behavior with treats, praise, and toys to build a strong, positive relationship between you and your pet. We do not use punitive methods like shock collars or choke chains.

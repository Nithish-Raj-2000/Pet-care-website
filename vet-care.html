Advanced Medicine

Specialized Treatments

Beyond routine checkups, our clinic is equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology to handle complex health issues.

Digital Radiology

Advanced X-ray imaging for precise diagnosis of internal injuries, bone fractures, and organ abnormalities.

Ultrasound

Non-invasive imaging to evaluate heart conditions, abdominal organs, and pregnancy monitoring.

General Surgery

From routine spays/neuters to complex soft-tissue surgeries, performed in our sterile surgical suite.

Orthopedics

Treatment for joint and bone conditions, including cruciate ligament repair and fracture stabilization.

Cardiology

Advanced ECG diagnostics, cardiac monitoring, and evidence-based heart disease management plans tailored for your pet.

Dermatology

Expert diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions, coat disorders, and allergy testing for lasting comfort and health.

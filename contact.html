Common Questions

Before You Contact Us

How do I book an appointment?

Use our online form above, call +1 (800) 123-4567, or visit us in person during business hours.

What are your response times?

Emails are replied to within 24 hours. Phone calls are answered immediately during business hours.

Is there an emergency line?

Yes — for after-hours emergencies we partner with a 24/7 local animal hospital. Call our main line for the direct number.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have a dedicated parking lot with 20+ spaces at our facility. Street parking is also available nearby.

Can I bring multiple pets?

Absolutely! We welcome all household pets. Just let us know how many you're bringing when booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and cash. Pet insurance claims can be filed at checkout.
